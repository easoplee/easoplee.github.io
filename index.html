Hi! I am a first-year PhD student in the General Robotics Lab at Duke University, advised by Boyuan Chen. My research focuses on the exciting intersection between robotics and machine learning. I am currently tackling multi-robot multi-task manipulation tasks using learning-based methods. Ultimately, I am driven to develop robots that can intelligently and seamlessly interact with humans and the environment.
